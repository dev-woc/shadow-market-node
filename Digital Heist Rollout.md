# The Digital Heist - Rollout Plan

## Overview
A 20-scenario narrative arc teaching LeetCode Easy concepts through an e-commerce heist story. Each scenario is a "mission" that requires solving a specific algorithm problem.

---

## The Narrative Arc

### Act 1: The Infiltration (Scenarios 1-5)
| Mission | Codename | LeetCode | Concept |
|---------|----------|----------|---------|
| 1 | The Gatekeeper | Two Sum | Hash Map |
| 2 | The Infinite Money Glitch | Contains Duplicate | Hash Set |
| 3 | The Wash | Best Time to Buy/Sell Stock | Sliding Window |
| 4 | The Bot Swarm | Majority Element | Voting Algo |
| 5 | The Secret Knock | Valid Palindrome | Two Pointers |

### Act 2: The Expansion (Scenarios 6-10)
| Mission | Codename | LeetCode | Concept |
|---------|----------|----------|---------|
| 6 | The Takeover | Merge Two Sorted Lists | Linked List |
| 7 | The Glitched ID | Missing Number | Bit Manipulation |
| 8 | The Leaderboard | Search Insert Position | Binary Search |
| 9 | The Paper Trail | Remove Duplicates | Two Pointers |
| 10 | Cooking the Books | Move Zeroes | Two Pointers |

### Act 3: The Counterstrike (Scenarios 11-20)
The whistleblower arc - defensive/forensics scenarios.
